Module 04
Story Making
Data storytelling — guiding stakeholders from raw numbers to clear, actionable insights using a narrative structure with annotated visuals.
Finding 01

Cardiology generates the highest revenue per patient

With an average bill of ₹85,000 and a 7.2-day stay, the Cardiology department outperforms all others by 34%. Complex procedures and longer recovery drives both metrics.

Finding 02

Re-admissions are concentrated in Oncology

62% of all re-admitted patients belong to the Oncology ward — flagged in dark red across all reports. Targeted post-discharge follow-up protocols could reduce this by an estimated 20%.

Finding 03

March 2026 saw a 20% admission spike

Seasonal respiratory illnesses combined with a surge in orthopedic referrals pushed admissions to 265 — the highest single month in the dataset. Staffing plans should anticipate this annually.

Recommendation

Department-level filtering cuts report review time by 60%

Enabling the department prompt in the Reporting module reduces report length significantly for department-specific reviews, saving clinical managers an average of 8 minutes per session.
